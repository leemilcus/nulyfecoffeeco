About Us

WHERE IT ALL STARTED

Welcome to the world Nulyfe Coffee Co, where we serve 'coffee to remember' and raise the finest baristas around.

Our journey began as a thought back in 2021, its pronounced Newlife then we just twisted up the spelling to make it unique and draw young people's attention. As the new generation in the coffee industry, we have passion for spreading knowledge to the youth in order to help them get employment easily. If your resume has atleast one course it's easier to get the opportunity to work because you bring value to that company, they wont spend time and resources on training.

That is why at Nulyfe Coffee we provide Barista Courses as our main service of business.

OUR BARISTA TRAINING

We offer industry-leading, rigorous training programme that consists of theoretical and practical instructions, equipping every single student with the skills and passion to deliver a consistent, authentic coffee experience to thousands of people every day, across Africa and beyond.

Our courses equip students with the tools and knowledge to thrive in a busy customer-centric environment.
So whether you end up working in a small coffee shop or for a big busy restuarant, this is the course for you!
